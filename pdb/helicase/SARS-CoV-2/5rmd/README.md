# PDB 5rmd

## Overview

**Protein name**: helicase

**Organism**: SARS-CoV-2

**Method**: X-Ray Diffraction

## Description

Crystal Structure of the SARS-CoV-2 helicase in complex with Z57614330. Its ligands are zinc ionszinc ions, phosphate ions and N-ethyl-4-[(methylsulfonyl)amino]benzamide.

## Basefolder

5rmd.pdb and 5rmd.cif - the coordinates of the threedimensional molecular model

5rmd-sf.cif - deposited diffraction data for this PDB entry

5rmd.mtz - structure factors after automatic refinement of molecular model against the diffraction data.
